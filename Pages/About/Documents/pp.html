Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to the MirAI Privacy Policy. This privacy policy explains how MirAI ("we", "us", or "our app") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our AI chatbot service powered by the Google Gemini API.

Important Note: MirAI is a third-party application that uses the Google Gemini API. Your use of MirAI is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Conversation Messages: The content of messages you send through the chatbot.
  • Custom API Keys: If you choose to use your personal Google Gemini API key.
  • App Preferences: Theme, language, and AI persona preferences.
  • Image Files: Images you upload for AI analysis (if such a feature is available).

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Interaction logs, frequency of use, and types of requests.
  • Technical Data: Browser version, operating system, and device information.
  • Connection Data: Internet connection status and response times.

Important: We do NOT collect sensitive personal information such as full name, email address, phone number, or other personal identification data unless you voluntarily provide it in conversation with the AI.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the AI chatbot service.
  • Process your requests through the Google Gemini API.
  • Store conversation history locally on your device (optional).
  • Improve user experience and app functionality.
  • Manage user preferences (theme, language, AI persona).
  • Detect and prevent service abuse.
  • Display connection status and performance indicators.

3. Data Storage

3.1 Local Storage

MirAI stores the following data locally on your device using browser storage technology:

  • Conversation History: Stored in your browser's localStorage.
  • User Preferences: Theme, language, and other settings.
  • Custom API Keys: If you use them.

3.2 Third-Party Server Storage

Your messages are sent to the Google Gemini API for processing. Google may store this data according to their privacy policy. We have no control over data storage on Google servers.

Encryption: Backup files (.medf) are encrypted using AES-GCM standards with random salt and IV to protect your data.

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties, except for the following:

  • Google Gemini API: Your messages are sent to this API for processing.
  • External Search Services: If you use the /search feature with external search engines (SearchAPI.io, Google Search, etc.), your search queries will be sent to those service providers.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or a valid government request.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

MirAI uses browser storage technologies (localStorage, sessionStorage) to store preferences and conversation history. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or cross-site tracking technologies.

For push notification functionality (if enabled), we use OneSignal which may use their own tracking technologies.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption for backup files (.medf format).
  • Local storage on the user's device.
  • Recommended use of personal API keys (not shared public keys).
  • Signature validation for backup files to ensure data authenticity.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

7. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access your conversation history through the export/backup feature.
  • Deletion: Delete conversation history at any time via the clear chat button.
  • Preference Control: Change privacy settings through the settings panel.
  • Opt-out: Stop using the application at any time.
  • Data Portability: Export your data in .mdf or .medf format.

8. Children's Privacy

MirAI is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via:

  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/Allwaysever/MirAI/issues
  • Project Page: https://allwaysevermirai.netlify.app/about

For questions regarding the Google Gemini API, please refer to Google AI Terms of Service.

11. Governing Law
